Overview

About WET

HumidiFi is an active liquidity DEX (prop AMM) on Solana. HumidiFi’s vision is to make Solana the home of the world’s most efficient, responsive, and transparent markets: true internet capital markets. The protocol redefines decentralized trading by merging on-chain execution with institutional-grade market-making logic, creating a system where liquidity is always active, adaptive, and optimized for performance. Since launching in June 2025, HumidiFi has become Solana’s leading DEX, processing more than $1 billion in daily volume and often achieving over 35 percent of all spot trading activity on the network (source). The total addressable market includes every trader using Solana’s major DEX aggregators, all of which route flow through HumidiFi. HumidiFi is evolving alongside Solana in a way that few projects can. The protocol’s architecture adapts in lockstep with the network’s technical advancements, leveraging every improvement in speed, memory efficiency, and validator infrastructure to push on-chain markets forward. $WET is built to power HumidiFi, the liquidity engine for internet capital markets and DEX for DeFi 2.0.
