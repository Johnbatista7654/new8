Space: The First Leveraged Prediction Markets on Solana

Overview
 Space is building the first leveraged prediction market on Solana, with support from Kalshi and the Solana Foundation. Users globally can trade real‑world outcomes (crypto, politics, sports, tech, culture) with up to 10x leverage. The product combines elements of Hyperliquid and Polymarket and is live in Alpha, with public launch targeted for Q1 2026.

Space runs a CLOB (Central Limit Order Book) with 0% maker fees, directly addressing the liquidity issues typical in prediction markets. While Polymarket has already validated over $20B in demand, Space aims to capture that market on Solana with leverage as its core differentiator.

Pitch deck: https://docsend.com/view/qjcvkyqw8bx8anrp

Key advantages:

  • Proven demand for prediction markets

  • First leveraged prediction market on Solana

  • Up to 10x leverage on outcomes

  • Team with prior project scaled to $1.5B market cap

Token Value Capture

  • 50% of all platform revenue used for $SPACE buybacks and burns

  • Creates deflationary pressure and direct value capture for token holders

With gamified loops, liquidity incentives, and partnerships (Kalshi, top NFT communities), Space is positioned to be a defining Solana protocol.
 Forecast: $2–4B trading volume by Year 3, implying $20–40M annual revenue (1% avg fee), with half flowing to buybacks and burns.

The founding team previously built and exited UFO Gaming, demonstrating strong product delivery and community-building in web3. They plan a pre-product TGE in Q4 2025, using a playbook they’ve executed before.



Token Sale Round

Platform & Product

Core innovation:

  • Brings real leverage to prediction markets:

    • Traditional PMs: max ~100% return

    • Space: up to 10x leverage → up to 1000% returns on binary & multi-outcome markets

Market structure & fees:

  • CLOB for deep liquidity, instant fills, transparent pricing

  • Makers: 0% fees + liquidity rewards

  • Takers: dynamic 0.02%–2% fees

User retention & gamification:

  • Hyperliquid‑style engagement:

    • Seasonal airdrops, daily streaks, achievements, referrals, social rewards

    • Trading points → token distributions and rewards

Developer ecosystem:

  • Space is open infrastructure: teams can build tools and derivatives on top, making it more protocol layer than just app.

Partnerships & growth:

  • Kalshi collaboration (institutional validation & support)

  • NFT partnerships: Neo Tokyo, Market Elites, more in pipeline

  • Affiliate and revenue-sharing programs

  • Cookie.fun InfoFi campaigns to accelerate user growth

  • Growth engine staffed with talent from Spotify, Nike, Aston Martin

  • Pyth as official finance oracle provider

  • Moonpay as official onboard payments provider

  • Helius as official RPC/node provider

Space combines:

  • 10x leverage

  • Buyback & burn tokenomics

  • Strong incentive design

  • Global, permissionless access

→ A capital-efficient, trader-focused prediction market.



Market Overview

Prediction markets are now proven:

  • Polymarket: $20B+ volume

  • Kalshi: $10B+ volume

Remaining issues:

  • Capped upside, shallow liquidity, poor execution, wide spreads

Rain.one shows the upside of “next-gen” PMs:

  • RAIN FDV ≈ $8.8B

  • ~$28M recent 24h volume

  • Demonstrates that modern UX + strong tokenomics can attract serious speculators quickly.

Space targets the capped-upside gap:

  • First platform to provide true leverage on outcomes

  • Faster, more capital-efficient structure for both retail and sophisticated traders

  • Global, permissionless, execution-focused

Competitors (Polymarket, Kalshi, niche PMs) do not offer leveraged outcome trading, leaving whitespace. By combining leverage with gamification and Solana performance, Space aims to be the next evolution of prediction markets.



Tokenomics Overview

Balanced design to align ecosystem, liquidity, and contributors:

  • Ecosystem – 48.5%
     Incentives, rewards, community programs, airdrops to drive adoption and long-term engagement.

  • Liquidity – 10.0%
     For CEX/DEX liquidity and stable trading.

  • Listings – 8.0%
     Exchange listings and integrations.

  • Marketing – 5.0%
     Acquisition, brand, and promo. Early campaigns already show strong execution.

  • Core Contributors – 15.0%
     Team and key contributors for long-term alignment.

  • Investors – 13.5%
     Early backers and strategic partners; moderate share of supply.



Investment Case

Prediction markets are now high-volume, durable businesses with global appeal. The missing piece has been:

  • Leverage

  • Deep liquidity and tight spreads

  • Capital efficiency on a modern, scalable chain

Space addresses this by:

  • Launching natively on Solana

  • Introducing genuine leverage to outcome trading

  • Using a CLOB to fix liquidity and execution quality

  • Implementing strong value capture (50% revenue → buyback & burn)

  • Layering in advanced engagement mechanics and credible partnerships

The founding team’s proven track record (UFO Gaming) and existing backers/support reduce typical early-stage execution risk.

For investors, this round offers:

  • Early exposure to a first-mover leveraged PM on what is arguably the cycle’s most vibrant L1

  • Entry before public ICO and before the broader narrative shift to leveraged prediction markets

  • A realistic path to becoming the flagship outcome-trading protocol on Solana

In a market with:

  • Validated demand

  • Growing user appetite

  • Few teams with the technical + operational chops to execute

Space stands out as a high-upside, asymmetric bet on the next generation of prediction markets.
